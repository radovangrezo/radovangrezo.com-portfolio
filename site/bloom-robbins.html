Bloom Robbins

Hair: Beautiful, Campaign Effect: Even More Beautiful

The Brief

Bloom Robbins had social media and influencer marketing down to a science. But they'd hit a ceiling — the combined reach of influencers, Instagram, and Facebook couldn't unlock the next wave of customers. For further growth, they needed a proper brand campaign.

The Work

We presented several concepts. The client was brave enough to pick the weirdest one: a mesmerizing animation style paired with a striking audio track that burrows into your brain and refuses to leave. The kind of ad people actually comment about — not to complain, but to say they can't stop humming the song.

The Results

On YouTube, 98% of viewers watched the skippable 30-second spot all the way to the end. That's not a typo.

After airing across Slovakia and Czechia — on TV sponsorship, via text messages, YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, banners, and newsletter — bloomrobbins.cz saw a 170% month-on-month boost in new visitors. And thanks to the campaign's activation layer, those new visitors converted at virtually the same rate as existing customers. Not just awareness. Revenue.
