Raiffeisenbank

Winning a large pitch and redefining the brand's communication for years to come.

The Brief

Win the pitch. Then prove the idea works in market.

The Work

Every bank in the country was trying to out-funny the next — stuffing spots with jokes, punchlines, and winking charm. We zagged. We made Raiffeisenbank the first to take your money seriously, built around a simple insight: earning it wasn’t all smiles and laughs, so maybe the bank that looks after it shouldn’t pretend it was.

The Results

The client liked the thinking enough to award their business to DDB Prague in the pitch — and then actually ran the campaign. In advertising, that’s three wins in one: win the room, win the account, win the air.
